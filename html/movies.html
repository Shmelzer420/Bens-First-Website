My Top 5 Favorite Movies!

The End Of Evangelion

The animation is amazing and it has a very interesting way to physically manifest human emotions and struggles with beautiful scenes, watch it!!!

The Shining

Incredible cinematography and fantastic acting in a very strange and intriguing environment make this movie a must watch!

Apocalypse now

arguably the best "Vietnam" movie of all time with one amazing set after another, thought provoking commentary on war and the morality of the vietnam war, very important movie.

Evil Dead 2

This horror movie is just dumb fun! it doesn't take itself too seriously but also has some really scary moments, watch this if you like horror movies.

Whiplash

This Incredible movie is all about Jazz and the struggles of being an artist trying to make it big, very good soundtrack and amazing actors!
